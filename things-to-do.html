Things to do

STAYING IN WATERFORD

If you decide to spend a few extra days in Waterford, Ireland's oldest city, here are a few things to do within the city:

  • Waterford Treasures - Five museums within minutes walk of each other:
    1. Viking 3D Experience
    2. Medieval Museum
    3. Bishops Palace
    4. Museum of Time
    5. Silver Museum

      6. (Combined tickets are available)

  • Reginalds Tower
  • Waterford Crystal - World Famous Crystal Making Factory
  • Geoffs Pub and Revolution are good places for food and drink.

FURTHER AFIELD

Between 20-60 minute drive to:

  • The greenway and walk or hire bicycles
  • Mahon Falls
  • Mount Congreve Gardens
  • The Suir Valley Railway
  • Tramore (Shaina's home town and seaside)
  • Dunmore East (fishing village)

STAYING IN TRAMORE

If you decide to spend a few extra days in Tramore, Shaina's home town, here are a few things to do around the town:

  • Walk the beach and the sand dunes - one of Ireland's longest beaches
  • Donerail Walk and Coastguard Cafe - overlooks Tramore Bay
  • Newtown Cove Walk
  • Lafcadio Japanese Gardens
  • Join the sea swimmers at the Guillamene, Newtown Cove if brave enough!
  • Group Surfing - A number of surf clubs in Tramore
  • O'Neills (Good Dinner Food) and the Vic Pub - Check facebook for music nights
  • Buy fresh sourdough bread, cafes, coffee from Seagull Bakery
