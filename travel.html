Driving in Ireland is very similar to driving in UK.
Please take extra care on country roads as they are often narrow with sharp corners, slow-moving tractors, and no street lighting.
Speed signs are also in kmh rather than mph.
Your current UK car insurance should cover driving in Ireland; separate cover shouldn't be needed.
However, make sure to check any limitations with your current insurance provider.
See Driving in Ireland | Ireland.com.
