Reception

Following the church ceremony, our wedding reception will be held within the 18th-century, restored country mansion of Faithlegg House Hotel's Minaun Ballroom.
The 200-acre Faithlegg estate is steeped in history, with the present house built in 1783 by the Bolton family (see history of Faithlegg).
From the hotel entrance, the Minaun Ballroom is easily located from the hotel's main reception.
Hotel guests also have access to the Leisure center. There is also an 18 hole championship golf course on-site.
There are easily accessible ground floor hotel rooms right next to the Ballroom, with a lift upwards to the other hotel rooms also directly outside.
There is also a large free car park next to the entrance of the hotel.
